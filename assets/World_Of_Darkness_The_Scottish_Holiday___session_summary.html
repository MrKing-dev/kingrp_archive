9/24/2021 Players: Ethan, Allison, Jack, Jeremiah, Grace. Background knowledge was provided - The story is set in Scotland, in the highlands in the town of Inverness. The vampire prince in Inverness is name Ian and has a history with Ethan's character. The highlands are wild and are the home to werewolves and other creatures of the night - vampires stay close to home when they can help it. There is a battlefield nearby called Culloden, where many Scotsmen died trying to take back their country over 200 years ago. There is also a significant hill with standing stones nearby called Clava Cairns - these are host to a large group of mages who use them for their purposes. The group of vampires this story revolves around own a pub on the eastern outskirts of Inverness. This acts as a small source of income for them, as well as a cover. They have a haven underground, beneath the basement. They try to keep the clientele to regulars, and few, when they can to avoid attracting notice. The Gang has a haven beneath the basement of the pub. At a point in the past, they tried expanding the haven, only to end up connecting to an abandoned series of subway tunnels. Their haven now includes these tunnels and a few abandoned subway cars. It is late evening, after the sun has set. The pub, about the size of Mr. King's classroom, is cozy with artificial lighting. 8-9 small tables and a medium bar contain a total of about 8 regulars, two small families, and 1 loud, boisterous newcomer. The Gang plans to close early to make a scheduled pickup at a local distillery. This is normal practice for them. The scene begins with Max and Becca behind the bar, Zahir in a corner, avoiding people, and Moonshine in the basement.
