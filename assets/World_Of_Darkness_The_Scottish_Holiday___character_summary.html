Becca’s Character Questionnaire (I answered as Becca, because that makes the most sense in my mind): What do you perceive as your greatest weakness? While it is also possibly one of my greatest strengths, I consider my friendliness to be my greatest weakness. I have a bad habit of letting people closer to me than I ought to, and I’m honestly surprised it hasn’t lead to my downfall yet. Some day, I worry that I will try and befriend the wrong person, and it will either destroy me or hurt someone I care about. Do you have any scars, tattoos, or birthmarks? Yes, actually! I have a small scar on my left palm from the time I slipped while I was learning how to carve wood with my dad. It wasn’t that serious, and it didn’t even need stitches, but the cut was apparently deep enough that it left a mark. I also have a tattoo on my ankle. It reads “H+S” in simple calligraphy. I got it a few months after my parents died, to remind me that they would want me to keep going without them. I have no birthmarks.
13:17
Is your real reason for becoming an adventurer different from what you tell others? If someone were to ask me why I stick with the team even though Ian is no longer a factor, I would probably tell them that it was because I committed to take care of them. While this is true, I would probably never tell them that a large part of it is that I never want to be lonely again. Spending time with the team reminds me of the time before I was a vampire, back when I could help other people and be a better person. Once I lost my parents and couldn’t go back to being an EMT, I thought I was going to go insane. I would never willingly put myself into that position again. What was your family life like? My family was always very busy. My parents owned the pub, and made sure it was open every night that it could be. That meant that they couldn’t spend as much time with me as they wanted to, but they tried. My dad was especially good at finding time to spend with me, which resulted in a deeper bond between us. I loved my mother just as much, though. Overall, we had a healthy and happy dynamic between us, even though doing things as a family sometimes presented challenges.
13:18
Do you currently have a best friend whom you would protect with your reputation or your life? I would like to think that, although they aren’t really “best friends” persay, I would give up my reputation and my life for my team. I don’t even think that it would even be a difficult decision to make if I were making it for Roaring Waters or for Max, to be honest. The others would be more difficult to sacrifice for, but I would never leave it off the table entirely. Do you have any great rational or irrational fears or phobias? If so, what are the origins of or reasons behind them? Obviously, there are a few things that scare a vampire to the point that they lose themselves, such as fire. I’m not counting those for this question, because they just make sense and I can’t exactly help it. What I would consider to count, though, is one of my greatest rational fears: my fear of boats and deep water. I think the fear stems from the accident that killed my parents. I can’t really go into water anymore without thinking about how much they must have struggled as they died. I’ve seen and resuscitated drowning victims over my years as an EMT, and it was never pleasant. Asphyxiation isn’t a good way to go. Now mind you, I don’t just keel over in terror when I see a boat or water; in fact, I still enjoy observing bodies of water and the boats that sail on them. It’s the getting into the deep water or getting onto a boat that is the hard part. It won’t ever cause an issue for the team, though. I’m too strong to let my fears take me down. Most of the time.
13:18
Were you forced into your profession by parents or peers? If we are discussing my time as an EMT, the answer would be a resounding no. In fact, my parents encouraged me to do whatever I wanted to do in my younger years. I decided pretty early on in life that I wanted to help people, and that I wanted to go into the medical field. They supported me as best they could through my decisions. Their only expectation was that of my current job: I would take over the pub once they became unable, or as soon as I felt ready to take that step- whichever came first. I used to resent them for that decision, being forced to work in a place that was full of so many memories for me so soon after they died. I got over it, though. I’m not sure I would trade it for anything anymore. Did you leave a Will? Yes. When my parents died, I realized how quickly life can be taken away, and made a Will. I do not have any surviving family, so originally I left the pub and my belongings to one of my old EMT friends. When the team formed, I had it modified and made it so that my possessions will go to Max if I die. I figure he is the one who would know best how to use my assets for the greatest good. What kind of threat do you present to the public? Some would probably say very little, and that is the truth- right now. However, I know that if things were to go terribly wrong, I would probably be a force to be reckoned with. Especially with the new abilities I have under the Ordo Dracul, I would probably be a menace to human civilization if anything were to cause me to snap. I just have to hope that never happens, and if it does, that someone puts me down before I can cause too much damage.
